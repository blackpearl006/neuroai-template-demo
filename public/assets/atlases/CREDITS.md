# Atlas credits & sources

Parcellated atlases (`<key>.glb` mesh + `<key>.nii.gz` label volume) were
generated by `scripts/build-parcellation-meshes.py` from public sources, in
MNI152 space. The per-region `score`/`sig` values are **placeholder demo data**
(seeded random, top ~20% flagged important) — replace with your own.

| Atlas | Source | Reference |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Brainnetome-246 | existing mesh + neurodata/neuroparc | Fan et al. 2016, *Cerebral Cortex* |
| Schaefer-400 | nilearn `fetch_atlas_schaefer_2018` | Schaefer et al. 2018, *Cerebral Cortex* |
| AAL-116 | neurodata/neuroparc | Tzourio-Mazoyer et al. 2002, *NeuroImage* |
| Harvard-Oxford | nilearn `fetch_atlas_harvard_oxford` | Desikan et al. 2006; FSL |
| Yeo-7 | nilearn `fetch_atlas_yeo_2011` | Yeo et al. 2011, *J. Neurophysiol.* |
| Glasser-360 | neurodata/neuroparc (split L/R) | Glasser et al. 2016, *Nature* |

Coordinate-node atlases (no mesh) come from the BrainGraph-derived CSVs in
`/atlases` (Watson 2024): AAL-90, Dosenbach-160, Power-264, Craddock-200,
Gordon-333, Desikan-Killiany-82, Destrieux, HCP-MMP-360.

Sources:
- nilearn datasets — https://nilearn.github.io/ (atlases retain their original licenses)
- neuroparc — https://github.com/neurodata/neuroparc
- TemplateFlow — https://www.templateflow.org/

Please cite the original atlas papers above when you use these in research.
